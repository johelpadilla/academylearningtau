# Domain: Complex Systems / Finance — Volatility Regimes

## 1. Context

Markets exhibit **volatility regime shifts**, contagion, and asset synchronization. Although they are not "living systems" in a biological sense, they share the structure of **coupling transitions** in multivariate series.

## 2. EWS and Classical Metrics

- Realized volatility, VIX, rolling correlations of returns.
- Good for description; often **reactive**.
- Regime ML (HMM, etc.) predicts without offering interpretable ordinal decomposition like Φ₁–Φ₃.
