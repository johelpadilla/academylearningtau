# Frequently Asked Questions (In-Depth Answers)

## Is Systemic Tau just a Kendall tau with marketing?

No. The kinship with rank statistics (Kendall and the like) is real in the **ordinal substrate**, but the object of τ_s is the **coupling reorganization dynamics** in windows, often multiscale and coupled to the RECD. A static rank correlation coefficient does not define a clock nor Φ₁–Φ₃ levels.

## Why isn't the sign of Δτ_s / Δexcess3 always positive?

Because the metric measures **reorganization**, not "proximity to a threshold in a 1D potential." In VF, AF, or pacing, the system can **gain or lose** synergistic structure depending on the context. The evidence relies on **magnitude + surrogates + concordance**, not on a universal sign.

## Can I use it with a single variable?

The core is multivariate (N≥2). If you only have one series, the platform can build a proxy \(X=[z(x), z(|\Delta x|)]\) (CCTP pattern). This is a legitimate compromise, not magic: it makes the level–variation relationship explicit.

## Does this predict sudden death or a dengue outbreak?

**Not as a certified clinical/operational device.** It is a research and teaching framework. Any prospective use requires external validation, threshold calibration, and ethical governance.

## How does it differ from Transfer Entropy?

TE estimates **directional information flow**. RECD/excess3 estimate **levels of ordinal conjunction and synergy** oriented toward an emergent clock. They are complementary: the Full Lab can show both.

## Why Bandt–Pompe and not SAX or other symbols?

For parsimony, monotonic invariance, and a mature ecosystem (permutation entropy, paradigm papers). SAX or other alphabets are possible extensions; they are not the standard of the CCTP pilot.

## What should I cite?

1. The paper/preprint of the domain you use (e.g., CCTP for cardio).
2. The software (`systemictau`, `nested-recd`, this platform).
3. The original dataset (PhysioNet, LTER, etc.).
